idealo.de - Comparison Pages

Goal

Customers on idealo.de wish for a better comparability of the products on the site. Products from the same category should be presented clearly and easily comparable.

My Role

Working together with the marketing team I was responsible for the concept, the prototype and the final design of the project.

Used Tools

Pen & paper, Sketch (concept), InVision (user test), Sketch (design)

Concept

The old comparison pages of the idealo magazine were to be revised and modernized. Even the comparability and clarity left much to be desired on the old comparison pages.

The redesigned comparison pages should feature the following topics: responsive sites for desktop and mobile viewports, straightforward comparability, badges that show the cheapest option and the product with the best price-performance ratio.

UX Test

After we all agreed on the concept I transformed it into a prototype using Sketch and InVision. A user test was conducted to verify the concepts created. A total of five test persons took part in the test and went through a scenario in which they were asked to compare products with each other. During the test we particularly observed the handling of the table and the perception of the symbols and graphics.

The scenario was tested on both end devices. While the users had no problem to navigate on the desktop page and compare products rather, easily they were facing challenges on mobile. So after the test the mobile variant was updated to deliver the best possible experience.

Design

A few iterations and a bit of polishing later, the design was done and ready to be developed. For the implementation I was able to work really close with the developer in charge.

Through this cooperation it was possible for us to quickly identify minor problems, which could then be adapted directly by me. It was also exciting to see the features - like the fixed header - live, because you could only imagine it in the designs before.
